Intercity+ High-Speed Train

Overview and Purpose

Ukraine’s Intercity+ trains, launched by Ukrzaliznytsia in 2012, provide a high-speed rail option connecting major cities across Ukraine. Intercity+ services were introduced to modernize rail travel and reduce travel times, making long-distance rail journeys between key cities faster and more convenient for business and leisure travelers. With speeds up to 160 km/h, Intercity+ is a preferred choice for efficient and comfortable travel.

Features and Specifications

Intercity+ trains are typically powered by electric locomotives and are operated in cooperation with Hyundai Rotem and Tarpan, featuring modern amenities such as climate control, Wi-Fi (available on select routes), and a variety of seating classes, including First Class with more legroom and spacious seating.
The carriages are equipped with ergonomic seats, luggage racks, and restrooms, with some offering dining services where passengers can purchase meals and refreshments. The interiors are designed to provide a comfortable experience even on longer journeys, supporting Ukraine’s growing demand for reliable and modern rail travel.

Routes and Service Coverage

Intercity+ services cover essential routes, connecting major urban centers such as Kyiv, Lviv, Kharkiv, Dnipro, and Odesa. The network provides a fast, direct link between Ukraine’s economic and cultural hubs, significantly reducing travel times on high-demand routes like Kyiv to Lviv and Kyiv to Odesa.
These trains also support travel between central, eastern, and western regions of Ukraine, offering flexibility with multiple departures daily on popular routes. For travelers seeking to explore Ukraine, Intercity+ provides a reliable option to visit destinations across the country.

Current Status and Future Plans

Ukrzaliznytsia is actively working to expand and upgrade its Intercity+ services as part of a broader rail modernization initiative. Plans include expanding the fleet with new rolling stock, extending services to additional routes, and incorporating eco-friendly technologies to reduce emissions. Future developments aim to increase the frequency of services and improve onboard connectivity options like Wi-Fi and real-time travel updates. Ukrzaliznytsia’s modernization efforts reflect Ukraine’s commitment to offering a comfortable, efficient, and sustainable high-speed rail network.
